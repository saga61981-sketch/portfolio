About Me

Hello! I'm Saga Mohamed

I am a dedicated Turkish Translator and Interpreter with a strong academic background in Oriental Languages (Turkish & Persian) from Fayoum University

My expertise includes professional translation, cultural interpretation, and facilitating communication between Turkish and Arabic speakers. I have completed certified Turkish language courses at the prestigious Yunus Emre Institute in Cairo and gained valuable experience through volunteer work at the University Center for Career Development.

Currently, I am expanding my skillset into the world of web design and development. As a beginner in this field, I am passionate about creating responsive, user-friendly websites that combine aesthetic appeal with practical functionality. I'm enthusiastic about learning modern design tools and coding practices to build digital solutions that make an impact.

I believe in continuous learning and professional growth, constantly seeking opportunities to bridge language, culture, and technology.
